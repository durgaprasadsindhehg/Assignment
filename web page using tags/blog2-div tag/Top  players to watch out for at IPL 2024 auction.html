Top Players to Watch Out for at IPL 2024 Auction

Mitchell Starc

Mitchell Starc has made himself available for the 2024 IPL a long time ago and all the teams want to sign him. He is the best left arm fast bowler in the world and the pace and accuracy he has is simply incredible. He had stellar performances in the 2023 World Cup Semi final and final and was an important member of the winning team. He can also bat lower down the order as he has shown in the semi final of the World Cup by winning such a high pressure match for Australia.

Pat Cummins

The 2023 World Cup winning captain will also be on high demand. In 2023 alone Pat Cummins won the World Test Championship, Retained the Ashes and won the 2023 ODI World Cup. He is among the best fast bowlers in the world and he will be bought for a lot of money. A few teams might be looking for an overseas fast bowler but also a solid backup in the leadership department. He can also bat lower down the order as was visible 2 seasons ago when he made 2 mind blowing half centuries for KKR.
