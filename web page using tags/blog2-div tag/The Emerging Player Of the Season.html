The Emerging Player of the Season-Yashaswi Jaiswal

Jaiswal - who has been one of the most exciting young batters in the domestic circuit - has been one of the most consistent players in the tournament. He also became the fourth batter under 25 years of age to score 600-plus runs in a single season of the competition. Jaiswal finished the league stage of the IPL 2023 with 625 runs, averaging 48.08, and a strike rate of 163.6. His score of 124 is also the highest score by an uncapped player. He's also the second youngest cricketer to score 600 runs in a season.
